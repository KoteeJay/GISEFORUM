Level 1

Introduction to Subsea Engineering

Starting: November 15th, 2024

This course provides a foundational understanding of subsea engineering, focusing on the design, installation, and maintenance of equipment used in underwater oil and gas production. Participants will learn about subsea systems, offshore platforms, pipelines, and the challenges of operating in deep-water environments.
