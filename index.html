About Us

Pioneering Subsea Engineering for a Sustainable Future.

Welcome to the Global Institute of Subsea Engineering Forum (GISEFORUM), a premier platform dedicated to advancing the field of subsea engineering. We provide world-class training programs, events, and forums designed to equip professionals with the latest knowledge and skills in subsea technologies. Whether you're new to the industry or a seasoned expert, GISEFORUM offers a diverse range of opportunities to grow, collaborate, and innovate.


Our Mission & Vision The Global Institute of Subsea Engineering Forum (GISEFORUM) is committed to developing world-class professionals equipped to lead and innovate in subsea engineering. Our mission is to offer cutting-edge training and promote collaboration within the subsea industry, driving technological advancements and sustainable practices. Our vision is to be the leading forum for subsea engineering excellence, fostering a global network of experts and innovators.

UPCOMING TRAINING PROGRAMS

Level 11

Introduction to Subsea Engineering.

Starting: November 15th, 2024

This course provides a foundational understanding of subsea engineering, focusing on the design, installation, and maintenance of equipment used in underwater oil and gas production. Participants will learn about subsea systems, offshore platforms, pipelines, and the challenges of operating in deep-water environments.
