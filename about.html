ABOUT US

About Us

Pioneering Subsea Engineering for a Sustainable Future

Welcome to the Global Institute of Subsea Engineering Forum (GISEFORUM), a premier platform dedicated to advancing the field of subsea engineering. We provide world-class training programs, events, and forums designed to equip professionals with the latest knowledge and skills in subsea technologies. Whether you're new to the industry or a seasoned expert, GISEFORUM offers a diverse range of opportunities to grow, collaborate, and innovate.


Our History

Since its inception, GISEFORUM has played a pivotal role in advancing subsea engineering training and research. With decades of collective experience, we have built strong partnerships with industry leaders and institutions worldwide.


Global Reach

With participants from over 30 countries, GISEFORUM brings together a global community of subsea professionals. Our international partnerships span continents, ensuring our programs meet global standards and industry requirements.


Accreditations & Partnerships

GISEFORUM is accredited by the International Well Control Forum (IWCF) and works closely with leading subsea engineering companies like Shell, ExxonMobil, and TotalEnergies to deliver industry-relevant training

MISSION AND VISION

Our Mission: The Global Institute of Subsea Engineering Forum (GISEFORUM) is committed to developing world-class professionals equipped to lead and innovate in subsea engineering. Our mission is to offer cutting-edge training and promote collaboration within the subsea industry, driving technological advancements and sustainable practices.

Our Vision: Is to be the leading forum for subsea engineering excellence, fostering a global network of experts and innovators.
